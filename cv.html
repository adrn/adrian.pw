I'm a Ph.D. candidate and NSF graduate research fellow at Columbia University.

I'm interested in dark matter halos, galaxy formation, large surveys, and most data analysis and inference problems. I provide open access to my code and writing, and contribute to open source projects such as Astropy and matplotlib.
